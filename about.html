Our mission is to ease the burden of providing everything for your little ones in the comfort of your homes.

Parenting is a rollercoaster of surprises, some joyful, some not so much. As parents, you work hard to raise your kids in the best way possible. And this means buying new clothes, toys, strollers and a bunch of stuff only to replace them because your kids keep outgrowing them. But, what if there is another way? Our answer is an e-commerce platform for parents by parents, providing an organised and verified marketplace to buy & sell new and preloved kids wear, toys, furniture and so much more. And, we’ve made it as effortless as it can be. Parenting is hard enough already! We know, we’ve been there.

Our Founder

Jessa Mae Cortez-Pelayo is the founder, mastermind and bossmom of Buy.Sell.Swap Baby Stuff. As a First-time Mom during Pandemic Season, it was a big struggle for her to find stuff for her coming baby. Most of the malls and Department Stores were closed and everything was expensive.

However, it was motherhood came calling that Jessa discovered first-hand the challenging reality of bringing up a child in the present day. The price of modern parenting, the money and the resources it guzzled, the toll it took on the environment; all of it made Jessa ask herself – Can’t there be a better way?

She found the answer in Buy.Sell.Swap Baby Stuff. A community-driven, preloved marketplace for kids that connects parents across the country to sell, shop, swap and spread the joy of sustainable living. Jessa created the website to be a one-stop destination where parents can enjoy quick, hassle-free experiences that’ll simplify their lives. Because she believes every child deserves the best, and so does every parent!
